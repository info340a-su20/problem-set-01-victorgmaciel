Vegeta

Vegeta is one of the last survivors of the saiyan race. Early in the DragonBall Z series he is protrayed as a stubborn, villainous character who will stop at nothing to obtain absolute power. It is only after being humbled by various fighters and Kakorat(Goku) that Vegeta morphs into the anti-hero that he is known for today.

  • Vegeta is one of the most powerful characters in the series
  • Although stubborn, he eventually gives to reason and helps defend Earth from various villians
  • He sacrifices his life to save others and that is heroic!
